About

This page is intended to store and organise all of my articles, which deliberately may follow no specific theme. As a recent graduate out of an engineering degree, I am well-versed in different technologies and have the ability (and luck) to learn about new topics fairly easily. I am trying out writing as a method to make my learning and knowledge more permanent than simply reading, listening and watching content. I hope, in the process, I am able to help others finding something they didn't know before.

About the author

Hi there, this is Marta on the keyboard. I'm a 20-something year old, with a recent "Engineering of something" degree, from a fairly popular, headline-making university located in London. You are bound to discover the details in some of my articles, or perhaps in my CV. The thing is, it doesn't really matter.

What really matters is that, I am enthusiastic about change in all its different forms. Perhaps I have a stronger inclination towards technological change, because I am more capable of evaluating the reality behind vague promises and the muted world-changers. But, at this point in my life, I am hungry for new ideas and seeing how they could develop. Especially in the current climate, where clever change is so important. I wanted to dedicate my time to promote the good and great ideas that could lead to a better future.
