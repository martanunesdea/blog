What's this mess?

If this is what you end up thinking after talking to yourself for a good 10 minutes, you're not alone.

I got into engineering for the optimistic idea that most problems impacting our population and our environment can be solved through the creative human intellect and technology. And, out of university and almost a year of working in the industry, I am still convinced that this is indeed the only way.

With a bit more free time at my disposal, I look forward to producing digital content that could inspire others to believe and pursue this same idea; only humans will save humans out of this mess.
